Grace Marks Eligibility Rules

1. Valid Certificates and Eligibility

Category Valid Certificate Eligible Year Maximum Grace Marks
Conference Paper Presentation Certificate of Participation from a Recognized Conference Any Year 10 Marks
Extracurricular Activities College/University Participation Certificate Any Year 5 Marks
Sports Events (University Level) Participation Certificate Any Year 10 Marks
Sports Events (State/National Level) Winner/Participation Certificate Any Year 15 Marks
Technical Event Participation Valid Proof from Organizer (Hackathons, Tech Fests, etc.) Any Year 10 Marks
Club Activities and Involvement Certificate from College Club Coordinator Any Year 5 Marks
Volunteering in Social Service Events Certificate from NGO/College Authority Any Year 5 Marks
Blood Donation Campaigns Government/College Certified Proof Any Year 5 Marks (Once per Year)

2. General Conditions

  • There is no Limit for the grace marks for a semester.
  • Grace marks can be for improving grades.
  • All certificates must be submitted before the end-semester exam registration deadline.
  • Fake or altered certificates will lead to strict disciplinary action.

3. Application Process

  1. Upload the scanned copy of the certificate in the Grace Marks Portal.
  2. Students must upload the required document for verification in their SharePoint folder, ensure it is accessible to all, and submit the SharePoint link in the Achievements form for verification by the Class Advisor.
  3. Submit the request information correctly to avoid rejections of your achievements
  4. The Class Advisor will verify the certificate.
  5. If approved, grace marks will be added automatically.
