Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions and learn how to use the system.

Student Help

1. How do I view my feedback history?

Log in to the system and navigate to the "Feedback History" section in your profile.

2. How do I submit feedback?

After logging in, go to the "Feedback" page, fill out the feedback form, and submit it.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Registering an Account

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page and complete the registration form to create your account.

Step 2: Providing Feedback

Navigate to the "Feedback" page, fill out the feedback form, and submit it.
