Class Advisor Help

1. How do I approve student achievements?

Log in to the advisor portal, navigate to the "Achievements" section, and approve the student’s achievements.

2. How do I view student grades and progress?

Go to the "Student Progress" section, and search for the student to see their grades and achievements.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Approving Achievements

Go to the "Achievements" section, select a student, and approve their achievements.

Step 2: Viewing Student Grades

Navigate to the "Student Progress" section, search for the student’s name, and view their grades and progress.
